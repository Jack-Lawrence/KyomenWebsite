Kyomen (虚面)

or 'Hollow Mask,' represents the empty vessels an Oni soul must inhabit to manifest its power.

Long ago, the world shed its true forms and learned to survive by wearing faces. The Oni who stands at its centre is not a demon of ruin, but a warden of balance.

Wishlist on Steam Demo on itch.io

Core Features

  • Mask-Driven Environment: Your current mask dictates the floor’s collapse patterns and the types of enemies that rise from the depths.
  • Dynamic Consumption: Combat requires using mask energy. Depleting your ammo forces a mask cycle, instantly shifting your environment and tactics.
  • Escalating Arena: Stay mobile as tiles flash and vanish. Difficulty scales with every floor cleared.
  • Combo-Based Scoring: Master the rhythm of the Fukai to maximize score and climb leaderboards.
